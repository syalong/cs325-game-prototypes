Puppy Panic Iteration

  • High Concept: The puppys need attention!! Make sure you give it to them, otherwise, they'll become sad.
  • Theme: pet shop owner
  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: arrow keys
    • Randomness: random
    • Genre: simulation
    • Play Style: achiever
  • Player Experience Goals: Players are expected to experience the joy or irritation of tending to puppies begging for their attention.
