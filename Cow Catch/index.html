Cow Catch

  • High Concept: The cows are acting weird lately. Catch them before they hurt themselves.
  • Theme: Cowboy herding cows
  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: Arrow Keys
    • Randomness: Cows are randomly generated
    • Play Style: Casual
    • Genre: Casual
  • Player Experience Goals: Players can expect to be at least slightly entertained.
  • Gameplay: Cows are randomly bouncing about and Cowboy Carl has no idea why. Lend him a helping hand by using your left and right arrow keys and help him catch those cows before they hurt themselves.
      Missing from this prototype:
    • Meteors are falling to Earth and while you have to help Cowboy Carl catch his cows, don't let him get a concussion.
    • Carl's Stress Bar
  • Prototype Goal:
    • Accurately increase/decrease stress when cows fall or when they are caught
    • Get cows to come off screen from the bottom and return there, instead of cows randomly raining from above
  • Features: When cows are caught, Cowboy Carl's stress goes down. If cows are missed, his stress goes up. Don't let his stress reach 100.
  • Setting/Premise: Cowboy Carl's farm is peaceful, until the cows start bouncing around.
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: Kids would probably better enjoy this game
  • Play Time: A minute
  • Strategies: Be quick to move under a cow when a cow is falling.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Background: Space Background
    • Player Character: Cowboy
    • Cows: Cartoon Cow
